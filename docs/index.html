I'm a Senior Software Quality Engineer based in Ontario, Canada with over 13 years of experience across banking, insurance, and wealth management. My day-to-day is building and maintaining automation frameworks in Java, Selenium WebDriver, and Cucumber BDD — that's my core stack. I also work with XCUITest for iOS testing and REST Assured for API automation.

But I'm not just an engineer. I independently built and shipped DayDrop — a native iOS app written in Swift. I run a YouTube channel where I edit every video myself using DaVinci Resolve and CapCut. And I produce original music in FL Studio, with two released singles.

I believe the best testers for creative tools are people who actually create. That's what makes my perspective different.
